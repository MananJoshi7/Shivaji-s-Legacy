Shivaji's Legacy

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the legendary Indian warrior king of the 17th century, rose from humble beginnings to establish the Maratha Empire in western India. Through his strategic military tactics, innovative governance, and unwavering commitment to his people.

Jijabai

Jijabai, a name etched in history, was a woman of indomitable strength and unwavering resolve. Born into a noble family in Maharashtra, India, during the 17th century, she possessed a spirit that would shape the destiny of a nation. Jijabai was not just a mother, but a pillar of inspiration for her son, the legendary Maratha warrior, Shivaji.

Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, also known as Sambhaji Bhosale, was the second ruler of the Maratha Empire in India. He was born on May 14, 1657, as the eldest son of the empire's founder, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.Sambhaji succeeded his father to the throne in 1680 and ruled until his death in 1689.
